The Solution: Triage, Transparency, Accountability

The data exists. The backlog is documented. Three interventions to clear the queue.

Severity-Based Triage Queue

Tickets open 1+ year should auto-escalate to emergency status. No heat in winter, no elevator for disabled residents — these cannot sit in queue behind paint complaints. Weight by vulnerability: elderly, children, disabled.

15 tickets open since 2020 — 5 years and counting

Fix HPD's Own Buildings First

HPD-managed buildings take 53.9 days vs 14.3 for private. The city cannot credibly fine landlords while its own buildings are 4x worse. Fund a dedicated rapid-repair team for the 943 HPD-managed buildings.
